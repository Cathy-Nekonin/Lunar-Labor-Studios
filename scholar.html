Scholarship Struggle

Free money, but at a cost...



Scholarship Struggle follows Skye as she tries to find a scholarship that really nabs her interest. On her journey, she also consults her friend Adri for advice on how to handle the situation. As you progress through the game, you can achieve 1 of 3 different endings to cap off Skye's story.

Scholarship Struggle was made for the OST Jam Vol. 5 Game Jam on itch.io.
