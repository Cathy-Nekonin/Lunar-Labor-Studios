Besting the Battleruler REMAKE

Defeat the tyrant and claim the throne!



In Besting the Battleruler REMAKE, defeat the tyrannical Battleruler who rules over the city and claim the throne for yourself! In the game, you train with the previous king to take down an oppressive dictator. In this world, it's survival of the fittest, and only the fittest become king! After defeating the Battleruler, explore his abandoned castle and claim the throne's seat for yourself!

Besting the Battleruler REMAKE is a remake of my very first video game made in RPG Maker Fes, fittingly named Besting the Battleruler.
